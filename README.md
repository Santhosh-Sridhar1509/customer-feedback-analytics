# Customer Feedback Analytics Platform

This repository contains representative Product Management artifacts
for an AI-assisted platform that transforms customer feedback into
actionable insights for product teams.

## Problem
Customer feedback is fragmented, reactive, and underutilized for
product decision-making.

## Solution
An AI-assisted analytics platform that clusters feedback, summarizes
themes, and highlights trends to inform product prioritization.

## Artifacts
- PRD: Product requirements and scope
- Metrics: Success metrics and guardrails
- AI Design: Model approach and explainability
- Risks: Product, data, and adoption risks

> Note: All artifacts are anonymized and representative.
