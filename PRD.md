# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Objective
Enable product teams to make data-driven decisions using customer
feedback at scale.

## Problem Statement
Feedback arrives through multiple channels and requires significant
manual effort to analyze, delaying insights.

## In Scope
- Text-based feedback ingestion
- AI-assisted clustering and summarization
- Insight dashboards for PMs

## Out of Scope
- Voice sentiment analysis
- Automated decision-making

## Functional Requirements
- Feedback ingestion pipelines
- Topic clustering
- Explainable summaries
- Trend comparison over time

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Explainability
- Data privacy and access control
- Scalability

## Dependencies
- NLP services
- Data storage platform
- Visualization framework
